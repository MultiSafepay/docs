## What it is
[E-Invoicing](https://www.multisafepay.com/e-invoicing) is a MultiSafepay branded billing solution enables merchants to take full control of their credit management process, the payment process and of contact with their customers. Post-payment methods such as E-Invoicing are very customer-friendly, as they allow paying for a purchase after its delivery. MultiSafepay offers a complete set of tools for automatically sending invoices and payment requests to the customer directly from your webshop.
