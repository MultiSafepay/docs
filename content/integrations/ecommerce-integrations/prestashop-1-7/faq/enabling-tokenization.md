To enable [tokenization](/tools/tokenization/):

1. Sign in to your PrestaShop 1.7 backend. 
2. Go to **Modules and services** > **Modules and services** > **MultiSafepay**.
3. Click **Configure**.
4. Set the **Tokenization** field to **YES**.
5. Click **Save**.
