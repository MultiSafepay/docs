In the [API](/api):

```
    "status": "",
    "financial_status": "",
```

`status`: Status of the webshop order. In your MultiSafepay Control, this is the **Order status**.

`financial status`: Payout of the transaction. In your MultiSafepay Control, this is the **Status**.
