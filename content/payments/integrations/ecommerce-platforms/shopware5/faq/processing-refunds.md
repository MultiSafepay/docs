You can process refunds for all Shopware 5 payment methods except [post-payment methods](/payments/methods/billing-suite) from your:

Refund rules:

- Full refunds from your [MultiSafepay account](/account/multisafepay-account/processing-refunds/) 
- Full refunds from your Shopware 5 backend
