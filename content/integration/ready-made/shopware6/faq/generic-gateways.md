The MultiSafepay plugin for Shopware 6 supports [generic gateways](/faq/general/generic-gateways/), e.g. for branded gateways. 

In your Shopware [backend](/glossaries/multisafepay-glossary/#backend), on the MultiSafepay settings page, enter the gateway code provided by MultiSafepay.
