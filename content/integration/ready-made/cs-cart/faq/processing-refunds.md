**Refund rules**  

- From your [MultiSafepay dashboard](/refunds/full-partial/): Full refunds
- From your CS-Cart [backend](/glossaries/multisafepay-glossary/#backend):  
    - Full refunds
    - Refunding more than the original transaction is **not** supported
