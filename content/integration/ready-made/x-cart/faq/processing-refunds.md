**Refund rules**  

- From your [MultiSafepay account](/refunds/full-partial/): Full refunds 
- From your X-Cart [backend](/glossaries/multisafepay-glossary/#backend):  
    - Full refunds
    - Refunding more than the original transaction is **not** supported
