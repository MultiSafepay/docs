## How to test

1. Under **Authentication** > **Query**, enter a **website** or a **terminal group** [API key](/docs/sites#site-id-api-key-and-security-code) from your **TEST** account.
2. Under **Path params**, enter the website or terminal group ID.
3. Click **Try it!**
