Which documents do I need to upload before a payment can be made?

Partner compensation can only be paid after the partner account has been fully activated. Before that you can make full use of the Partner Dashboard and you will receive partner compensation from connected merchants.

Follow the step-by-step plan in your partner account by going to activation and uploading the necessary documents.

  • Copy ID
  • Copy a bank statement or transfer 1 euro
  • UBO statement.

It takes an average of 3 working days before the account is fully activated.
