PSD2 Update

Payment Service Directive 2 (PSD2) is a set of laws and regulations etablished by the European Banking Authority. PSD2 aims to improve the security of online payments within Europe.

One of the requirements of PSD2 is the use of Strong Customer Authentication (SCA). This requirement ensures that online payments are performed with Two Factor Authentication (2FA). One of the protocols used to meet this requirement is called 3D Secure (3DS).

Starting January 1st 2021, the SCA requirement will officially be enforced. Please read our blog for more information on Strong Customer Authentication and what it means for your business.
