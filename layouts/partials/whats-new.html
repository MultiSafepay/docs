PSD2 Update

Starting January 1st 2021, the new Payment Service Directive 2 (PSD2) will officially be enforced. PSD2 aims to improve the security of online payments within Europe.

One of the requirements of PSD2 is the use of Strong Customer Authentication (SCA). This requirement ensures that online payments are performed with Two Factor Authentication (2FA). The protocol used to implement this requirement is called 3D Secure (3DS).

Please read our blog for more information on Strong Customer Authentication.
