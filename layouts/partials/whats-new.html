PSD2 update

The Payment Service Directive 2 (PSD2) is a set of laws and regulations established by the European Banking Authority. It aims to increase the security of online payments within Europe.

PSD2 requires Strong Customer Authentication (SCA) to ensure that online payments use Two Factor Authentication (2FA). One of the protocols we use to meet this requirement is 3D Secure (3DS).

For more information about SCA and what it means for your business, check out our blog.
